About the Book

"Danny the Dinosaur" takes readers on an exciting journey into history, exploring how dinosaurs were once called dragons and the fascinating interactions between humans and these mythical creatures. Learn how historical figures like Alexander the Great encountered these incredible beings and discover a new perspective on the world of dinosaurs.

Key Themes

  • Exploration of historical and scientific perspectives on dinosaurs.
  • Fun and engaging storytelling for children.
  • Inspiring curiosity about history and nature.

Perfect for Young Readers

Designed for children aged 6-10, "Danny the Dinosaur" combines fun illustrations and captivating stories to spark the imaginations of young readers while teaching them about history and science.
